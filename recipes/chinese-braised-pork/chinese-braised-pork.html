Chinese Braised Pork

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 bulb garlic (separated but not peeled)
  • 1 tsp peppercorns (cracked or smashed)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1½ lbs boneless pork ribs or pork shoulder, cut into bite size pieces (675g)
  • 3 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • ¾ cup water (180ml)
  • ½ tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 4 hard boiled eggs (peeled)

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a medium sized pot. Saute garlic cloves, cracked peppercorns, and minced garlic for 1 minute.

  2. Add pork and fry to seal in the juices. This should take about 3 minutes.


  3. Add sweet soy sauce, sugar, and water.


  4. Bring liquid to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes.


  5. Add salt and hard boiled eggs. Cover and simmer for another 15 minutes.


  6. Turn eggs occasional to coat evenly with soy sauce. Turn off stove.

  7. Serve warm with steamed rice.
